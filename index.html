About

The 2026 IEEE International Workshop on Biometrics and Forensics (IWBF 2026) will take place on April 23–24, 2026 at EURECOM on the beautiful Côte d’Azur (French Riviera). IWBF is an international forum devoted to facilitating synergies in research and development among multimedia forensics, forensic biometrics, and forensic science. State‑of‑the‑art research in biometrics, surveillance, multimedia forensics, and related areas will be showcased. The 14th edition of IWBF brings together practitioners and researchers—from industry, academia, and end‑users—working with advanced biometric technologies in forensic applications.
