Activities

IWBF 2026 will be held at EURECOM in Sophia Antipolis, in the heart of the Côte d’Azur. If you plan to extend your stay before or after the workshop, here are some suggestions of things to do nearby. The list below is ranked by approximate distance from EURECOM.

Things to Do Around Sophia Antipolis (ranked by distance)

1) Sophia Antipolis Nature Walks (≈ 1–3 km)

The Sophia Antipolis area is surrounded by green spaces and walking paths. Several short trails are accessible near the campus, offering an easy break in nature without needing transportation.

2) Valbonne Old Village (≈ 3 km)

Just a few minutes from EURECOM, Valbonne is a charming medieval village with a lively central square, narrow streets, and many cafés and restaurants. It is ideal for a relaxed lunch or dinner after the workshop sessions.

3) Antibes Old Town (≈ 7 km)

The historic center of Antibes features stone ramparts, a Provençal market, small boutiques, and seaside cafés. It is a great destination for an afternoon stroll or evening dinner.

4) Picasso Museum – Antibes (≈ 7 km)

Located in the Château Grimaldi, the Picasso Museum hosts works created by Pablo Picasso during his stay in Antibes, along with other modern art collections. The museum also offers beautiful views over the Mediterranean.

5) Chemin du Littoral – Cap d’Antibes (≈ 8 km)

The Chemin du Littoral is a scenic coastal path along the Cap d’Antibes, offering spectacular sea views, rocky cliffs, and access to quiet coves. It is ideal for a half-day outdoor activity combining nature and relaxation.

6) Juan-les-Pins Beaches (≈ 9 km)

Close to Antibes, Juan-les-Pins is known for its sandy beaches and seaside promenade. It is a perfect spot to unwind, especially if the weather is pleasant.

7) Cannes & La Croisette (≈ 18 km)

Famous for its film festival, Cannes features the elegant La Croisette boulevard, luxury boutiques, and the historic Le Suquet district overlooking the harbor.

8) Nice Old Town & Promenade des Anglais (≈ 20 km)

The city of Nice offers a vibrant old town, local markets, museums, and the iconic Promenade des Anglais. You can easily spend a full day exploring the city and enjoying the seaside.

9) Lérins Islands (≈ 20 km + boat)

From Cannes, short boat trips lead to the Lérins Islands, offering peaceful nature, historical sites, and crystal-clear waters. This is an excellent option for a half-day escape from the mainland.

10) Grasse – Perfume Capital of the World (≈ 20 km)

Located in the hills above the coast, Grasse is famous for its perfume industry. Visitors can tour traditional perfumeries and learn about fragrance production (and sometimes even create a personalized scent).
