Registration Fees

Please note that accepted papers should be presented by one of the authors as a condition for publication. At least one of the authors must register at the regular registration rate (i.e., no PhD/Master Student discounts) before the camera ready deadline. Each author registration covers only one paper. Additional authors can benefit from some discount.
